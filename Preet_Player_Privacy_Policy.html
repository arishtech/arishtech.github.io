Privacy Policy - Preet TV

Last updated: 2026-01-25

Overview

Preet Live Stream Player ("we", "us") provides an application that plays live streams from user-provided M3U playlist URLs. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your choices.

Data we collect

  • Advertising Identifier (Advertising ID): Not collected (no ad SDKs are integrated).
  • Device & app metadata (device model, OS version, app version): Collected for diagnostics and crash reporting.
  • Network information (connectivity status, IP): Collected to enable streaming and handle network errors.
  • Location: Collected only if you enable local device discovery (Cast). This is requested at runtime and not collected otherwise.
  • User-supplied M3U playlist URLs: Stored locally on the device (SharedPreferences) to load channels.
  • Diagnostics: Crash reports and performance logs may be collected for debugging and improvement.

How we use data

  • Load and play streams from the playlist URL you provide.
  • Troubleshoot crashes and improve app stability.
  • Support local device discovery (Cast) when you explicitly permit it.

Third parties

We do not integrate advertising SDKs by default. Diagnostic or analytics SDKs (if included) may receive device/app metadata; check the library documentation for details.

Storage and retention

Your playlist URL is stored locally on the device until you remove or replace it. Diagnostics are retained only as needed by the analytics/crash SDKs.

Your choices

  • You can remove or replace stored playlist URLs in the app settings.
  • You can revoke location permissions in system settings.

Security

We use standard Android storage and network APIs. We recommend using HTTPS playlist URLs; the app will prefer secure connections.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: <preettv@arishtech.com>.
